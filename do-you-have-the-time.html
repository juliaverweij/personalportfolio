    Do You Have the Time?

    A collaborative short film using found footage to question the concept of convenience. Do You Have The Time? aims to explore modern convenience through the lens of different ways to make a cake. The three methods are juxtaposed next to each other to highlight the ways in which some are faster, while others allow you to have more personal input into the final product.
    Together with Mars Yamaguchi and Isabel van der Stadt
