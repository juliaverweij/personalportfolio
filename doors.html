    Doors

    Starting from a fascination of the heavily decorated doors in Prague, Doors is a collaborative book that visually explores the differences and similarities between doors around Europe. This book is divided into three sections (Archive, Google Maps, Our own photography) and amplified with added text fragments, some mirrored to mimic the function of doors, readable only when viewed from the opposite side of the page.
    Together with Gilles Goosen, Jeong In Moon and Anne-Sophie Berting
