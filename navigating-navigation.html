    Navigating Navigation

    A website and zine design for Navigating Navigation, an exhibition hosted by the 3rd year GDA students that follow the Experiment&Motion elective. The zine, which folds out into a poster, was made in collaboration with Alexia Dinca, Adriada Florescu and Lars Sangers, and contains a short description written by each student about their work. The website contains embedded videos of all students work.
