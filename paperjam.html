    PAPERJAM

    PAPERJAM is a collaborative project that aims to open up a platform for students to sell their creative work at a fair price. This project started from a mutual excitement of wanting to sell our work and wanting to start building a network for ourselves. The previous year this translated into a website, this year we are going physical with a market.
