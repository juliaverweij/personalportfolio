    • (Un)familiar

    (Un)familiar is an exploration of our position in the physical world and how we behave and interact with our environment, presented through a physical collage of the process along with voiceovers speculating what it feels like to find our way through familiar and unfamiliar places. What is it that we look at when we are in (un)familiar places? How will we remember a place when we grow old? What about all the details?
